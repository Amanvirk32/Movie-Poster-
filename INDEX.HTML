Hypnotic

Genre: Thriller, Mystery

Director: Robert Rodriguez

Starring: Anya Taylor-Joy, Chris Hemsworth, Oscar Isaac

Plot: Hypnotic is a gripping thriller that follows the story of a young woman who discovers her extraordinary ability to hypnotize others. As she delves deeper into her powers, she becomes entangled in a dangerous conspiracy that puts her life and the lives of those she loves at risk. With stunning performances and intense suspense, Hypnotic will keep you on the edge of your seat until the final twist.
